Welcome to Kosho Interia ("Kosho Interia," "we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to our website, https://www.koshointeria.com/ (the "Site"). a) Personal Information You Provide:

  • Contact Information: Such as your name, email address, phone number, and physical address, which you may provide when filling out contact forms, requesting consultations, subscribing to newsletters , or otherwise communicating with us.
  • Project Information: Details about your interior design needs or project specifics if you choose to share them with us via forms or direct communication.

b) Information Collected Automatically:

  • Usage Details: As you navigate through the Site, we may automatically collect certain information about your equipment, browsing actions, and patterns, including details of your visits (like traffic data, location data logs ), resources accessed, and information about your computer and internet connection (IP address, operating system, browser type).

3. How We Collect Information

  • Directly from You: When you fill out forms, send us emails, or communicate with us directly.
  • Automatically: Through the use of cookies, server logs, and other similar technologies as you interact with our Site. We may use third-party services like Google Analytics to help us understand site usage.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To respond to your inquiries, provide information, schedule consultations, or provide services you request.
  • To manage and administer interior design projects if you become a client.
  • To improve our Site, services, marketing, and customer relationships.
  • To analyze trends and usage patterns on our Site.
  • To send you newsletters, promotional materials, or other information you may opt-in to receive (you can opt-out at any time).

  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

We value your privacy and do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: For any other purpose disclosed by us when you provide the information or with your explicit consent.

Our Site uses cookies to enhance functionality and analyze usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.
